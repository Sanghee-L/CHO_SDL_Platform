# CHO SDL Platform

Self-Driving Laboratory for CHO Cell Line Development

CHO SDL Platform is an AI-guided prototype for Cell Line Development (CLD) that integrates multi-objective optimization, digital twins, reinforcement learning, engineering simulation, and closed-loop self-driving laboratory (SDL) workflows.

The platform was developed to address a common challenge in biologics development:

> How can we identify high-value CHO clones earlier, eliminate false-positive candidates, reduce experimental burden, and accelerate development decisions?

---

## Key Features

- Clone screening and prioritization
- Multi-objective Pareto optimization
- CHO digital twin simulation
- Reinforcement learning-based process optimization
- Multi-omics-informed scoring
- Engineering intervention recommendation
- CRISPR risk assessment
- Experimental outcome simulation
- Closed-loop SDL learning engine
- Executive clone intelligence dashboard

---

## Platform Workflow
```
Synthetic Clone Population             ↓ Feature Engineering             ↓ Predictive Scoring             ↓ Pareto Optimization             ↓ Digital Twin Simulation             ↓ Reinforcement Learning             ↓ Candidate Selection             ↓ Engineering Recommendation             ↓ Risk Assessment             ↓ Experimental Outcome Simulation             ↓ SDL Learning Loop             ↓ Executive Decision Platform 
```
---

## Project Objectives

The platform focuses on five core objectives:

1. Prioritize promising CHO clones
2. Detect false-positive candidates early
3. Recommend engineering interventions
4. Reduce experimental burden
5. Enable continuous SDL learning
